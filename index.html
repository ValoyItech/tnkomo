Meet Our Director

Thabang Nkomo

Director & Senior Attorney

Thabang Nkomo is the founding director of T NKOMO Attorneys Inc, bringing over 15 years of extensive legal experience to the firm. With a passion for justice and a commitment to excellence, Thabang has built a reputation as one of the Eastern Cape's most trusted legal practitioners.

Specializing in civil and criminal litigation, property law, and Road Accident Fund claims, Thabang has successfully represented hundreds of clients across diverse legal matters. His approach combines thorough legal knowledge with practical solutions, ensuring clients receive both expert representation and personalized service.

Thabang holds an LLB degree and is admitted as an Attorney of the High Court of South Africa. He is a member of the Law Society of the Eastern Cape and regularly participates in continuing legal education to stay current with evolving legal practices and regulations.
